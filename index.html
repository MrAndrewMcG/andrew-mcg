Hi there!

My name is Andrew McGoveran, and I put together this site as part of my application for the Theme Support position. I currently work for Apple as a Genius, helping customers resolve problems with their devices, and completing hardware repairs on Macs and iPhones. Theme Support seems like the perfect opportunity to merge what I’ve learned at Apple with which I have been working on outside of work.

HTML / CSS / jQuery

I’ve always been passionate about technology, and over the last year and a half I have been building a working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in my spare time. I love that there is always something new to learn, and now I'm hooked on the challenges of front end development.

For this site I've used Flex Box for the layout, and Sass for styling. I used jQuery to add smooth scrolling to the navigation links, to hide and show content, bring the buttons to life. Below I've included a few examples of what I've been working on over the past few months.
